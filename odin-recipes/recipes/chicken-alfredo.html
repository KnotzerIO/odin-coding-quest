Chicek Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a classic Italian pasta dish that is made with fettuccine noodles, chicken, and a creamy Alfredo sauce. It is typically garnished with fresh parsley and grated Parmesan cheese and served with garlic bread or a side salad.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine noodles
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the fettuccine noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the chicken pieces with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned and cooked through.
  4. Add the garlic to the skillet and saut√© until fragrant.
  5. Reduce the heat to medium and add the heavy cream to the skillet. Bring to a simmer.
  6. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until melted and the sauce has thickened.
  7. Add the cooked fettuccine noodles to the skillet and toss to coat in the sauce.
  8. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. Divide the Chicken Alfredo among serving plates and garnish with chopped parsley and additional Parmesan cheese.
