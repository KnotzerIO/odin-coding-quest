Pad Thai

Description

Pad Thai is a popular Thai noodle dish that is made with stir-fried rice noodles, shrimp, tofu, or chicken, and a combination of vegetables and seasonings. It is typically served with a lime wedge, crushed peanuts, and fresh cilantro.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz rice noodles
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce
  • 1/4 cup tamarind paste
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2 cup firm tofu, cubed
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/4 cup chopped peanuts
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Steps

  1. Soak the rice noodles in warm water for 30 minutes, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the fish sauce, tamarind paste, and sugar. Stir until the sugar is dissolved.
  3. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add the shrimp and tofu to the wok and cook until the shrimp is pink and the tofu is golden brown.
  5. Push the shrimp and tofu to one side of the wok and pour the beaten eggs into the other side. Scramble the eggs until cooked through.
  6. Add the drained rice noodles and fish sauce mixture to the wok. Toss everything together until well combined.
  7. Add the bean sprouts and green onions to the wok and toss to combine.
  8. Divide the pad Thai among serving plates and garnish with chopped peanuts, lime wedges, and fresh cilantro.
